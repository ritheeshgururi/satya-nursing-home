About Us

Bringing Expertise in Women’s Health and Family Wellness

Since 1996, Satya Nursing Home has been committed to providing exceptional healthcare services to individuals of all age groups and economic backgrounds. Specializing in gynecology, obstetrics, and general health issues, we pride ourselves on offering state-of-the-art facilities that combine advanced medical technology with compassionate care. Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone, ensuring health, hope, and happiness for our community.

  • Specialized Care: Expertise in gynecology, obstetrics, fertility, laparoscopy and general health for all age groups.
  • Modern Facilities: State-of-the-art technology for quality healthcare.
  • Affordable & Compassionate: Delivering accessible, patient-centered care since 1996.

Dr. Gururi Ashok Kumar MD

(OBGY), FMAS

Dr. Gururi Ashok Kumar is a highly respected and experienced Obstetrician and Gynecologist with a passion for providing exceptional healthcare to women. As the founder of Satya Nursing Home, Dr. Ashok Kumar has dedicated his career to advancing women's health through a combination of expertise, compassion, and innovative care. With an MD in Obstetrics and Gynecology and a fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery (FMAS), Dr. Ashok Kumar is committed to offering the highest standard of medical services for women at every stage of life.

His mission is to create a healthcare environment where every patient receives personalized, professional care with a focus on their well-being. Under his leadership, Satya Nursing Home has become a trusted name for gynecological care, maternity services, and advanced surgical treatments. Dr. Ashok Kumar’s vision is to empower women with the knowledge and care they need to live healthier, happier lives.
